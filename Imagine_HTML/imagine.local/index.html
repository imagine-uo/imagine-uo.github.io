Welcome to the IMAGINE research group website!

The IMAGINE research group «IMprecision in the AGgregation of INformation provided by Experts» is a research group from the University of Oviedo (Spain) created in 2024. In this website you will find information about our research, the members of the group, our on-going projects and the scientific production, as well as updated information about our activities.
